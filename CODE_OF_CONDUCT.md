# Code of Conduct

Little Boy's Aegis is an open-source security project. We want the community to be useful, rigorous, and safe for contributors with different backgrounds, experience levels, and communication styles.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, then ask clarifying questions when something is unclear.
- Give technical feedback that is specific, actionable, and tied to the work.
- Credit ideas, reports, research, and prior art.
- Treat sensitive security information carefully.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Repeated disruption of issues, pull requests, discussions, or reviews.
- Sharing exploit details, credentials, production data, or vulnerability reproduction steps in public when responsible disclosure is required.
- Encouraging unauthorized testing or misuse of the project.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, limit participation, or block users when behavior harms the project or community. Enforcement decisions should be proportionate, documented where appropriate, and focused on protecting contributors and users.

## Reporting

For non-sensitive conduct concerns, contact the maintainers through the relevant repository issue or discussion with only the details needed to route the report. For sensitive conduct concerns or reports that include private information, use a private maintainer channel where available and avoid posting personal details publicly.
